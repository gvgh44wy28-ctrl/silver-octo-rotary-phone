Editable Deck Plan Model

Draw a simple top-down model for takeoff measurements. Drag corners to edit the deck shape. Tap Add Stair or Add Rail Run, then drag the new item into place. The model is added to the estimate PDF.

Editing: drag corner dots, snap points, foundations, stairs, railings, beams, and joist layouts. Select an item by tapping it, then rotate or extend it. Pinch with two fingers to zoom the model, and twist two fingers to rotate the model view by touch. Side handles expand along one axis at a time, and corner handles expand both the X and Y axis together. Stairs, railings, footers, piers, block foundations, beams, and joist layout zones can now be resized wider, narrower, deeper, or shallower directly on the grid. This is a planning model, not a permit drawing.
